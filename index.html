A Step Closer...

We are the to be engineers who are enthusiastic about creating real world projects according to industrial and societal requirements. We bridge problems with basic technologies to make our life easier.

We implement projects in the following domains:
Artificial Intelligence(AI), Machine Learning(ML), IoT, Image Processing, Automation and other state-of-the-art technologies. We also conduct introductory and advanced prototyping workshops by the industry for the students to get hands on experience in the above mentioned subjects.

We conduct competitions and hackathons for the students to get some learning experience. We do plan to attend techfests in various colleges.
